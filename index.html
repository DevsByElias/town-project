Visit Pori

Truly strange, absolutely unique

Pori is stubborn and repeatedly draws strength from creative madness and contradictions. At its core, the city conceals resilient entrepreneurship and a unique way of doing things

Top three activities to do in Pori

Immerse Yourself in Pori Jazz Festival

Pori Jazz Festival, a musical phenomenon, transforms the city into a harmonious haven each summer, bringing together diverse rhythms, world-class artists, and an electrifying atmosphere

Yyteri Beach, Your Oasis of Sun, Sand, and Sea in Pori

Yyteri Beach, a coastal gem near Pori, invites you to unwind in its golden sands and embrace the soothing melody of the Baltic Sea—a perfect blend of nature's beauty and seaside serenity.

Porin Metsä, a Labyrinth of Trails in Nature's Playground

Explore a green sanctuary where countless trails weave through nature's embrace, offering an outdoor haven for recreation and rejuvenation in the heart of Pori.
